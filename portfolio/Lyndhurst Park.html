Welcome

A country club
without the noise.

Lyndhurst Park is designed around good golf, good food and the feeling of being away from everything, without travelling far.

The course moves through heathland, pine, open meadow and old estate ground. The clubhouse is relaxed rather than formal, with dining rooms, a members’ bar and terraces overlooking the closing holes.

Explore the course
The Course

Championship detail.
Natural restraint.

The routing is simple, walkable and deliberately uncluttered. Fairways ask for position rather than power, while the greens reward patient golf and a good eye for pace.

It is premium without feeling over-produced: firm turf, quiet transitions, considered bunkering and views that do not need decoration.
