05 · FAQ

Questions, before the form.

The things we get asked most. If yours isn't here, the contact form takes about 60 seconds.

How long does a typical project take?
Most websites and stores ship in 4–8 weeks. Booking and automation builds usually take 2–4 weeks depending on integrations and volume of test data.
Shopify or WooCommerce — which one?
Both. Shopify is our default for product brands because checkout, payments and shipping are built in. WooCommerce works well when you already have a WordPress site you want to keep.
What does workflow automation actually look like?
A real example: new enquiry → enriched with company data → summarised by AI → pushed to CRM with a Slack alert. Or: booking → confirmation → calendar event → reminder the day before. Built in n8n so they're visible and editable.
Is the AI stuff actually useful?
When it's scoped. We build chatbots trained on your FAQs, AI email drafts, product description generators and enquiry summarisers. We don't pretend AI replaces strategy or design — but it's great at the boring bits.
Do you provide ongoing support?
Yes. Maintenance packages cover updates, content edits, automation tuning and bug fixes. You can also just hire us per-change at an hourly rate — no minimum retainer.
What if my business doesn't fit any of the four services?
Tell us what you need on the contact form. If it's adjacent — landing page, internal portal, dashboard, integration — we've probably built something similar before.
